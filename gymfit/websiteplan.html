Website Plan: Gym Fit

Gym Fit

Gym Fitness Club

This name represents a community-focused fitness club that aims to help members achieve their fitness goals through personalized training, group classes, and wellness activities. The name suggests reaching the peak of personal fitness.

Domain availability (optional): gym-fitnes.club

Purpose of the Site

The purpose of the Fitness Peak Club website is to serve as a central hub for members and prospective clients. It will provide:

  • Information about fitness programs and classes
  • Online membership registration and management
  • Details about trainers and wellness coaches
  • Resources such as fitness blogs, nutritional guides, and community events

Scenarios

  • What group classes are available this week, and what are their schedules?
  • How can I sign up for a personalized training program?

Color Scheme

The website will use the following colors:

  • Yellow (#FFC107): Used for headers, buttons, and accents to create a vibrant, energetic tone.
  • Black (#000000): Used for text and background to ensure readability and contrast.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for headers (modern and clean aesthetic).
  • Sans-serif: Used for body text (easy readability).

Wireframe Diagram

Below is a description of the wireframe structure:

Mobile View

- Header with logo and navigation menu (hamburger menu style).
- Featured section with an introduction and "Join Now" button.
- List of available classes and schedules.
- Footer with quick links and contact details.

Desktop View

- Header with logo and navigation bar.
- Main banner with promotional content and call-to-action buttons.
- Three-column layout for features: programs, trainers, and resources.
- Footer with navigation links, social media, and contact information.
